Fushimi Inari Shrine The 10,000 gates

The Fushimi Inari is one of the most visited spots in Japan! This is a great spot to go hiking and look for the hidden bamboo forest. Despite the high altitude, there are several cafes, tea shops, and noodle shops along the way.
Fushimi Inari has many attractions and spots of interest besides the 10,000 torii gates. You can find attractions just a short walk from the main shrine, like the famous Tofukuji Temple and the Gekkeikan Okura Sake Museum, which used to be a sake factory.
