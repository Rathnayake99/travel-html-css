Yasaka Pagoda

Legend believes that if you can crawl under the considerable stone, you will dispel evil spells forever.
In Kyoto, almost all couples take their wedding pictures near the Yasaka Pagoda, right next to Ninenzaka. You can also visit the historic Yasui Kompiragu shrine nearby. People often purchase colourful balls to make a wish or crawl under considerable round rocks to dispel bad luck.
Whenever I have guests, I always take them to this area, which is always a hit. Luckily, the place is not far from the Kiyomizu temple area and the Gion area, so you can easily add the Yasaka Pagoda to your itinerary.
