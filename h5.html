KINKAKUJI TEMPLE

The Kinkakuji Temple is the most famous and picturesque in Japan! Catching sight of the gold-covered structure is an almost surreal experience–but the building is only covered in gold leaf for the top two floors.
The temple is open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Make sure to arrive a few minutes before it opens so you can avoid any crowds! You can also visit around 4 p.m. since the tourists start thinning out before closing.
The Golden Pavilion is outside the city, and you can’t enter the building, so there’s not much to do besides strolling around the garden.
