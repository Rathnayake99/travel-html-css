Samurai & Ninja Museum with EXPERIENCE

The Samurai and Ninja Museum, located right next to the Nishiki Food Market, is a must-add to your itinerary. The museum showcases samurai armour, weapons, ninja outfits, and many artefacts. The primary ticket comes with free tours in English. There are optional experiences like training like a ninja or samurai, watching a samurai sword show, and even trying on costumes!
The museum also has exhibits from the Edo Period from samurai and ninja families, along with authentic katanas!
